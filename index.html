about me


My name is Chloe Sofia Encenzo, nice to meet you! ッ I am currently a Software Developer at Morgan Stanley, but at my core I am a Filipina woman driven to diversify the tech space that saturates my community. I have a passion for solving problems, eye for design, & an eagerness to improve.

My academic journey started at Skyline College and ended at UC Irvine with a degree in Computer Science and specialization in Information. I pursued Computer Science not only because it allows me to express my own logical thinking and creative process, but also because I wanted to embody the quote "Be the change you want to see in the world". Technology can allow for a spread of opportunity, resources, and capital. How we collect, manage, and use information is critical in today's world. The lack of diversity in the tech industry is evident in the products and services it produces. Data isn’t discriminatory, but project managers and engineers can be. I believe the underprivileged POC youth are the untapped source of innovation the tech world needs, and I hope to build a career that inspires them to step into their power.

Besides programming, I love cooking (& eating), creating Spotify playlists, and being outdoors! I'm always open to chat, so shoot me a message below or connect with me via LinkedIn!
